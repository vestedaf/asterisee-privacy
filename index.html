Privacy Policy

Last updated: July 7, 2026

Asterisee ("we", "us") is a daily horoscope app. This policy explains what we collect, why, and your choices. We keep it short because the app collects little and sells nothing.

The short version

  • You can use Asterisee without an account. Your sign and reminder settings are stored only on your device until you choose to sign in.
  • If you sign in, we store your account details and any readings you save so they sync across your devices.
  • We do not sell your data, show ads, or use third-party advertising trackers.
  • You can delete your account and all associated data at any time from Settings → Manage account → Delete account.

What we collect

Used without an account (stored on your device only):

  • Your selected zodiac sign
  • Your daily-reminder on/off setting and time

If you create an account (optional):

  • Contact identifiers you provide: phone number and/or email address
  • Optional profile details: name, birthday
  • Readings you choose to save ("saved readings" and favorites)
  • A technical account identifier

Automatically, to make features work:

  • If you enable the daily reminder, a device notification token so the reminder can be delivered
  • Basic technical data required to operate the app (e.g., app version, error diagnostics)

We do not collect precise location, contacts, photos, or advertising identifiers.

How we use it

  • To generate and display your daily reading
  • To save and sync your sign, settings, and saved readings across your devices
  • To send the optional daily reminder you turned on
  • To operate, secure, and improve the app

We do not use your information for advertising and we do not sell it.

Service providers

We use a small number of trusted providers that process data on our behalf, under their own security and privacy commitments:

  • Clerk — account sign-in and profile (name, email, phone, birthday)
  • Convex — database for your saved readings
  • Expo / Apple — app delivery, updates, and push notification delivery

Data retention and deletion

We keep your account data while your account exists. You can delete your account at any time in Settings → Manage account → Delete account, which removes your profile and saved readings from our systems. Uninstalling the app removes the on-device data (sign and reminder settings).

Children

Asterisee is not directed to children under 13, and we do not knowingly collect personal information from them.

Security

We rely on our service providers' industry-standard protections and limit the data we collect. No method of transmission or storage is 100% secure, but we work to protect your information.

Changes

We may update this policy; we'll revise the "Last updated" date above and, for material changes, provide notice in the app.
